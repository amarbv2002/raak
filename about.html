WHO WE ARE

We have a strong history in the wood business, building a reputation for manufacturing quality pellets and briquettes for many years. From the outset, we made a major commitment to produce the finest quality pellets—from our investment in machinery, materials, and processes, to the recruiting and hiring of skilled employees. Our dedicated work force takes great pride in delivering the best wood pellets to our customers every day! That's why you can have confidence in RAAK Biocorp "Premium Certified Pellets and briquettes".

Based in Bangalore (India), we, RAAK Biocorp, are a noted provider of Bio Fuels, Stove for Kitchens and Burner for Industries. The premium quality and eco-friendly products offered are delivered to clients only after rigorous quality checking done by our team of quality specialists. The bio fuel pellets are checked for their consistency in quality by measuring characteristics, such as ash content, dust, moisture content and ash melting temperature. Every single product is packed using quality material for secure shipment at our clients’ doorstep. We strictly follow ethical business policies and ensure fair deals to the clients spread across Indian markets.

Our wood pellets are made from sawmill residuals such as sawdust, hog and shavings in combination with residuals from harvesting operations and low-grade timber that has no other economic value. The combination of utilizing both sawmill residuals and grinding harvest site residuals to manufacture greenhouse gas neutral wood pellets has had a substantial positive impact on the environment.
