Benefits of Pellets and Briquettes

Eco Friendly Fuel as it is non hazardous, safe to use and store and pollution free with lowest emissions..
It is more economical than solid fuels (like LPG/Coal/Furnace Oil/Gas/husk/wood)
1 It of oil/diesel =2 kg of pellets
1kg of Ipg = 2.5 kg of pellets
1nm3 of gas= 1 kg of pellets
4.5 units of electricity= 1 kg of pellets.
6kg of steam = 1kg of pellets
1.6kg of rice husk= 1 kg of pellets for steam generation.
Distribution/Transportation cost is less than that of the solid fuels.
Reduced carbon emissions — much less than oil, log, coal or gas-fired burners Heat intensity is the same when compared to traditional fossil fuels
More price stability than many other forms of fuel
Pellet burners are extremely advanced and can operate up to a 95% efficiency rate Easy to switch to pellets as burners are similar to oil-fired burners
Pellets burn cleanly and more efficiently compared to coal, logs or wood chip Convenient and safe to store compared to gas, coal, oil
